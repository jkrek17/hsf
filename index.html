About this viewer

This app parses NOAA OPC High Seas Forecast text and draws gale, storm, hurricane-force wind areas, sub-gale winds, dense fog, freezing spray, and low centers on a map. It is for situational awareness, not navigation.

Data sources

  • Live — National Weather Service API (api.weather.gov): product type HSF, locations AT1 (Atlantic) and EP1 (Pacific). A descriptive User-Agent is sent as required.
  • Archive — Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM): list and retrieve historical text by UTC date. The ◀ / ▶ buttons step through issuances; at the first or last of a day they load the previous or next UTC calendar day (skipping empty days).
  • Basemap — CartoDB Light (OpenStreetMap data).
  • Land mask — Natural Earth 1:110m physical land (public/land.geojson), used only for water-area calculations.

Map and filters

Polygons are clipped to the Atlantic or Pacific forecast domain. Pacific longitudes follow the bulletin (often 0–360°) for display. Use Hour and Warning filters to limit forecast periods and warning blocks. Layer checkboxes toggle feature types. The side panel shows raw text, a parsed summary, and wind-area totals (sum of polygon areas in square nautical miles by gale / storm / hurricane — these totals do not subtract land).

GeoJSON export

Exports filtered features with metadata (ocean, valid time, filters, feature count). Wind polygons may include area_nm2 (total polygon) and area_water_nm2 (after subtracting land); both are rounded to whole nm².

Area formulas

Geometry is built with Turf.js on WGS 84. For the Pacific basin, longitudes > 180° are converted with λ′ = λ − 360° before area operations.

Area in m²: turf.area(polygon) returns geodesic area in square meters.

Square nautical miles: 1 nautical mile = 1852 m, so

1 nm² = 1852² m² = 3,429,904 m²

A_nm² = A_m² ÷ 3,429,904

Water-only area: merged land polygons are subtracted from each wind polygon (turf.difference), then the same m² → nm² conversion applies. The land layer is generalized; near coasts, water area is approximate. If the mask fails to load, water area matches total area for that feature.

Popups

Wind popups show Water area (nm²) when available, using the same land subtraction as export.

Technical stack

Static site: ES modules, vendored Leaflet and Turf under vendor/, no npm required. Serve over HTTP (e.g. python3 serve.py). See the repository README.md for full detail.
